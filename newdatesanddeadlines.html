Where it all Happens

1600 Pennsylvania Avenue- the home address of every president since John Adams in 1800. Here, the president puts his signature on laws and policies, has important meetings with visiting diplomats, and keeps up to date with the events in the nation. It's been the heart of American politics for generations, and continues to serve as an iconic symbol of our nation.
